3. **Credit Efficiency** - $0.12 vs our ~$0.45 average
4. **AI Agents** - Automated workflows without coding
5. **Multiple Search Types** - People, Company, Domain, Intent, etc.
6. **White Label** - Full reseller capability

#### What Revify Does Better
1. **Deep Research** - More thorough company research with citations
2. **Persona-Based Emails** - CFO, CEO, Pricing, Tech, Sales angles
3. **Research Synthesis** - AI synthesizes multiple sources into insights
4. **Pain Point Hypothesis** - Specific hypothesis about company challenges
5. **Intent Signals** - Detailed intent signal extraction
6. **GHL Integration** - Native, deep integration with HighLevel
